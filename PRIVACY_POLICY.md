# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 24, 2024**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Personal OS ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We DO NOT Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers.** All data remains on your device and, if you enable it, in your personal iCloud account.

Specifically, we do not collect:
- Personal identification information
- Contact information
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports (unless you opt-in through Apple's system)
- Any content you create in the App

### Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data locally on your device:
- Tasks and to-do items
- Project information
- Health and habit tracking data
- Financial portfolio data
- News bookmarks and RSS feeds
- Social media content drafts
- Code snippets and notes
- App preferences and settings

### iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:
- Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings

## Third-Party Services

### API Keys You Configure

When you configure third-party API keys (optional):
- **News API**: For fetching news articles
- **GitHub API**: For syncing project data
- **Stock Price APIs**: For portfolio tracking

These API keys are:
- Stored securely in your device's Keychain
- Never transmitted to our servers
- Used only to make requests directly to those services
- Subject to those services' privacy policies and terms

### HealthKit Integration

If you grant HealthKit permissions:
- Health data is accessed only with your explicit permission
- Data remains on your device and in your iCloud Health data
- We do not collect or transmit health data to any server
- You can revoke permissions at any time in iOS Settings

## Data Security

### Local Storage
- All sensitive data is stored using iOS secure storage mechanisms
- API keys and credentials are stored in the device Keychain
- Data is protected by your device's passcode and biometric authentication

### Network Security
- All network requests use HTTPS encryption
- Certificate pinning is implemented for critical connections
- We implement security best practices to protect data in transit

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access and Control
- All your data is accessible within the App
- You can export your data at any time
- You can delete all data by deleting the App

### Data Deletion
To delete your data:
1. Delete the App from your device
2. If using iCloud sync, disable it in iOS Settings before deletion
3. Optionally, delete iCloud backup data through iOS Settings

### Opt-Out Options
- iCloud sync: Disable in iOS Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud
- HealthKit access: Revoke in iOS Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices
- Notifications: Disable in iOS Settings > Notifications > Personal OS

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice in the App
- Requiring acceptance of new terms for material changes

## Data Retention

- iCloud data follows Apple's iCloud retention policies
- No data is retained on our servers (we don't have servers)

## International Users

The App is designed to work worldwide. All data processing occurs on your device or in your iCloud account, subject to Apple's data handling practices.
